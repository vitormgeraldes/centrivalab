Target applications

NF membrane screening

Rapid comparative evaluation of membrane behaviour under centrifugal pressure conditions for membrane selection, benchmarking and formulation work.

RO-oriented feasibility studies

Compact experimental workflows for early-stage assessment and ranking of membranes and conditions relevant to higher-pressure separations.

Rotor-native method development

Screening campaigns designed around existing centrifuge infrastructure rather than pump-based rigs, reducing setup burden in early R&D.

Standardisation and data generation

Structured cartridge families and reproducible operating envelopes support method harmonisation, membrane comparison and future high-volume experimental data generation.
