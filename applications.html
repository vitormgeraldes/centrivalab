Applications

Application areas where low-volume, rotor-native membrane screening offers practical value.

NF membrane screening

Comparative evaluation of membrane behaviour under defined centrifugal pressure conditions for membrane selection, benchmarking and formulation work.

RO-oriented feasibility studies

Compact studies for early-stage membrane and condition ranking in higher-pressure process development workflows.

Standardisation workflows

Structured cartridge families and reproducible operating windows support more comparable screening datasets across campaigns.

High-throughput method development

Parallel cartridge operation helps accelerate experimental iteration while using standard centrifuge infrastructure.

Who this is for

The most credible audience framing is membrane scientists, process engineers and development teams who need technically meaningful screening without the full burden of pump-based bench systems.
