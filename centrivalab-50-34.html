CentrivaLab 50/34

Orthogonal centrifugal membrane cartridge for 50 mL conical tubes. The 50/34 configuration is designed for fixed-angle rotors around 34° and translates centrifugal hydrostatics into a defined transmembrane pressure envelope for membrane screening workflows.

  • Nominal feed: 13 mL
  • Pressure range: 2–60 bar equivalent
  • Rotor speed: 3,000–20,000 rpm
  • Membrane area: 1.45 cm²

Validated NF screening behaviour

  • Published validation work showed hydraulic permeability differences of only 7–12% relative to a reference NF crossflow unit, depending on membrane type.
  • Comparable permeation fluxes and rejection values were obtained for representative NF solutes, especially for neutral molecules and divalent salts.
  • Concentration polarization remains moderate and becomes low in industrially relevant flux windows.

Physics of operation

The transmembrane pressure generated by the cartridge is governed by centrifugal hydrostatics. In practical terms, rotor speed, fluid density and fill geometry determine the pressure envelope acting across the membrane. This supports reproducible operating-window definition and clearer comparison between screening conditions.
