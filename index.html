Why it matters

The O-CMF concept was designed to address non-uniform transmembrane pressure and concentration polarization issues associated with conventional centrifugal membrane filters, while enabling compact low-volume workflows and easier membrane replacement.

Uniform-pressure intent

The membrane is positioned orthogonally to centrifugal acceleration to support more controlled pressure distribution across the active area.

Low-volume operation

Published work reports membrane areas around 0.95 cm² and sample volumes below 10 mL in the validated scientific system.

Crossflow relevance

Validation against a SEPA CF Cell showed comparable hydraulic permeability, flux, rejection and concentration-polarization behaviour across representative NF conditions.
