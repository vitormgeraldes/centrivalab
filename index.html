CentrivaLab™

High-throughput centrifugal membrane screening platform

CentrivaLab is a centrifugal cartridge platform for rapid screening of nanofiltration (NF) and reverse osmosis (RO) membranes under controlled hydrodynamic conditions.

The platform is based on the orthocentrifugal membrane filtration (O-CMF) principle.

Applications

  • High-throughput membrane screening
  • Concentration polarization studies
  • NF / RO membrane development
  • Comparative testing of membrane materials and operating conditions

Available formats

CentrivaLab-50

Compatible with 50 mL centrifuge-tube rotors.
Typical sample volume: up to 15 mL.

CentrivaLab-100

Compatible with 100 mL centrifuge-tube rotors.
Typical sample volume: up to 20 mL.

Materials

  • Acrylic resin
  • PEEK
  • PES

Scientific basis

The O-CMF method provides a controlled framework for membrane screening and concentration-polarization analysis in centrifugal systems.
