High‑Throughput Centrifugal Membrane Screening

CentrivaLab is a rotor‑matched cartridge platform for rapid screening of nanofiltration (NF) and reverse osmosis (RO) membranes under controlled hydrodynamic conditions, based on the Orthogonal‑Centrifugal Membrane Filter (O‑CMF) principle. It is designed to support low‑volume, high‑information membrane evaluation workflows with realistic concentration polarization behaviour.

Current Standard Configuration

CentrivaLab 50/34
Designed for 50 mL fixed‑angle rotors around 34°. Suitable for NF/RO screening campaigns and custom development projects.

Additional rotor‑matched cartridges are under development for larger tube formats (~94 mL) and other angles.

Technology

CentrivaLab leverages the O‑CMF principle, where centrifugal force drives permeation through a flat‑sheet membrane positioned for uniform pressure and reproducible hydrodynamics. The concept was introduced in peer‑reviewed research and validated for scaled‑down membrane screening, with CFD‑supported evidence for controlled concentration polarization and rotor‑matched performance.
