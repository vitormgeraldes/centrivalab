Product platform

CentrivaLab™ products are organised as a scalable cartridge platform. Commercial names remain simple and stable, while deeper engineering identifiers evolve internally for traceability, revision control and manufacturing governance.

Commercial configurations

CentrivaLab 50/34

Current reference configuration for 50 mL conical tubes and fixed-angle rotors around 34°, especially JA-20 class 8 × 50 mL rotors. Designed for high-pressure NF/RO screening workflows in standard centrifuge infrastructure.

Status: commercial reference configuration.

CentrivaLab 94/25

Planned bench-top configuration for 94 mL rotor-class geometries around 25–26°, targeting Sigma 4 × 94 mL and 6 × 94 mL fixed-angle rotors. Designed to extend the platform into compact laboratory centrifuge workflows at 15,000–20,000 rpm.

Status: planned / under development.

How product architecture is managed

  • Brand: CentrivaLab™
  • Technology: O-CMF
  • Configuration: 50/34, 94/25
  • Engineering identifiers: managed internally for traceability and revision control

Materials and build logic

Commercial pages should not overcommit to a single public material stack. Depending on the product maturity and application envelope, builds may involve acrylic prototype resins, PEEK, PAEK, PPS and other supporting materials defined in the corresponding specifications and documentation.
