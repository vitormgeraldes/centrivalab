Publications

Peer-reviewed scientific support for the Orthogonal Centrifugal Membrane Filter (O-CMF) principle and the CentrivaLab platform.

Peer-reviewed papers

Journal article

Designing Centrifugal Membrane Filters with uniform-pressure for UF/NF/RO separations

Authors: Vítor Geraldes, Patrícia Henriques, Maria Diná Afonso, Ana Maria Alves, Rita F. Pires, Mónica Faria, Bart Van der Bruggen, Miguel Rodrigues

Journal: Journal of Membrane Science 702 (2024) 122752

DOI: 10.1016/j.memsci.2024.122752

This paper introduced the O-CMF concept, described the uniform-pressure design logic, and reported CFD-supported analysis showing nearly uniform concentration polarization and homogenization of the concentrate chamber by centrifugal convection.

Journal article

Ortho-centrifugal membrane filter for scaled-down high-throughput screening of nanofiltration membranes

Authors: Patrícia Henriques, Ana Maria Brites Alves, Vítor Geraldes

Journal: Separation and Purification Technology 378 (2025) 134458

DOI: 10.1016/j.seppur.2025.134458

This paper validated O-CMF against a SEPA CF Cell benchmark using two NF membranes and standard reference solutes, reporting comparable hydraulic permeabilities, fluxes, rejection values and concentration-polarization behaviour.
