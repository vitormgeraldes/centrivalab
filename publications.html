Publications and scientific support

This section is reserved for papers, preprints, technical notes and validated scientific communication related to O-CMF and the CentrivaLab platform.

Suggested structure

  • Peer-reviewed article introducing the O-CMF principle
  • Application note comparing centrifugal screening versus conventional approaches
  • Future studies covering membrane classes, operating envelopes and calibration logic

Communication rule

Academic outputs should prioritise the scientific term O-CMF. The commercial name CentrivaLab may appear as the product platform based on that technology.
