Publications and scientific support

This section brings together peer-reviewed scientific communication supporting the O-CMF technology and the CentrivaLab platform.

Peer-reviewed articles

Designing Centrifugal Membrane Filters with uniform-pressure for UF/NF/RO separations

This work introduced the O-CMF design, demonstrated near-uniform transmembrane pressure and analysed concentration polarization through experiments and CFD. It also developed transferable engineering correlations for mass transfer and concentration-polarization prediction.

Ortho-centrifugal membrane filter for scaled-down high-throughput screening of nanofiltration membranes

This work validated O-CMF as a scaled-down high-throughput NF screening platform against a reference crossflow unit, showing comparable hydraulic permeability, permeation flux, rejection and concentration-polarization behaviour across representative conditions.
