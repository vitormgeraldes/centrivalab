Orthogonal centrifugal membrane cartridge for 50 mL conical tubes. The 50/34 configuration is designed for fixed-angle rotors around 34° and translates centrifugal hydrostatics into a defined transmembrane pressure envelope for membrane screening workflows.

Physics of operation

The transmembrane pressure generated by the cartridge is governed by centrifugal hydrostatics. For a fluid with density ρ rotated at angular velocity ω, the pressure drop across a flat membrane positioned at radius rm relative to the free surface radius rs can be expressed as:

Δp = 1/2 · ρ · ω² · (rm² − rs²)

This equation supports reproducible pressure prediction and clearer operating-envelope communication.
